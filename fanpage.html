BIO

James DeWitt Yancey or better known as J Dilla, was born February 7, 1974 in Detroit, Michigan. He was the eldest brother to Martha, Earl and John. Much like his older brother, John later began making music by the handle of Illa J.

Eary in his career Jay Dee & MC Phat Kat formed a group 1st down. 1st Down was actually the first Detroit Hip Hop group to be signed by a major label (Payday Records). The deal quickly ended after their single, when the label went under.

Sadly, three days after the release of his album Donuts, J Dilla passed away due to cardiac arrest.
